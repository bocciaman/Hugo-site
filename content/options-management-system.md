### A structured workflow for managing credit spreads, CSPs, and covered calls — including 21-DTE management and performance analytics.

Most retail options traders:

- Track trades inconsistently  
- Guess when to manage positions  
- Don’t measure capital efficiency  
- Don’t track return vs annualized return  
- Lose structure over time  

This system replaces scattered spreadsheets with a single structured workflow.

## What This System Helps You Do

✔ Track credit spreads and wheel trades in one place  
✔ Monitor open collateral and capital deployment  
✔ Manage positions at 21-DTE with clarity  
✔ Measure ROC, Annualized ROC, and Profit Per Day  
✔ Track captured profit and long-term performance  
✔ Evaluate trade efficiency before entering a position  

## What’s Included

- **Dashboard** (active management view)  
- **Put Credit Spread Tracker**  
- **CSP & Covered Call Tracker**  
- **Performance Analytics sheet**  
- **Return Calculator**  
- **Position Sizing reference tab**  
- **Start Here workflow guide**

## Who This Is For

- Income-focused options traders  
- Wheel strategy traders  
- Credit spread sellers  
- Traders managing multiple positions  
- Traders who want structure and consistency

## How It Works

1. Log each trade in the appropriate tracker.  
2. Monitor the Dashboard weekly.  
3. Use 21-DTE alerts to manage positions.  
4. Track efficiency using ROC and Annualized ROC.  
5. Review long-term performance in the analytics sheet.  

This system helps bring structure, discipline, and clarity to your options trading workflow.
